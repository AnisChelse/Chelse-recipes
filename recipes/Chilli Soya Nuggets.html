Chilli Soya Nuggets

Description

This is an interesting vegetarian substitute for Chilli Chicken. This 0riental-style recipe with soya nuggets and capsicum can be served with fried rice or even paranthas

Ingredients

  • MAGGI Magic Cubes Vegetarian (Crumbled)
  • MAGGI Hot & Sweet Tomato Chilli Sauce
  • Soya Nuggets
  • Water
  • Oil
  • Dry Red Chillies
  • Onion
  • Green Capsicum
  • Green Chillies (Slit, Lengthwise)
  • Ginger Paste
  • Salt (To Taste)

Steps

  1. Prepare ingredients as mentioned in the list Soak soya nuggets in hot water along with ½ tablespoon salt for 30 mins. Squeeze out the water and keep aside.
  2. Heat the oil in a karahi and add dry red chillies, onions, capsicum, green chillies and ginger paste, garlic paste and sauté on medium heat till onions turn brown.
  3. Drain the soaked chunks and reserve the water. Squeeze them gently to remove excess water and add to the pan, add salt and mix well. Stir fry for about 5 mins.
  4. In the reserved water add crumbled MAGGI Magic Cubes – Vegetarian, soya sauce, tomato puree and tomato sauce. Mix well and bring to a boil on high flame, add to the pan with the soya nuggets and mix. Lower the heat and cook covered for 8-10 minutes.
  5. Garnish with the spring onions and serve hot with fried rice or noodles.
