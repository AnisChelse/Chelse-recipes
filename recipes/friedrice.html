Fried Rice

Description

A big bowl of Chicken Fried Rice never lets you down, because it is tasty, healthy and super-quick to put together! One of the best part about this rice stir-fry is that you can easily customize it by adding your favourite vegetables, which also adds more colour, nutrition and texture to the dish. For a Schezwan Twist, add scrambled eggs or omelette, cut into strips.

Ingredients

  • MAGGI Magic Cubes Chicken (Crumbled)
  • Oil
  • Garlic (Crushed)
  • Red Chilli Flakes
  • Spring Onion (Chopped)
  • Chicken (Boiled and Shredded)
  • Cooked Rice
  • Egg (Beaten)
  • Salt (To Taste)
  • Pepper Powder

Steps

  1. Prepare ingredients as mentioned in the list.
  2. Heat the oil in a wok, add the garlic and stir-fry it till fragrant. Add the red chilli flakes and spring onion (keep some spring onion aside for garnishing), sauté for 4-5 minutes.
  3. Add the chicken and sauté for a few more minutes, then add the rice and mix well. Sprinkle the crumbled MAGGI Magic Cubes and mix well.
  4. Season the eggs with salt and pepper and make into scrambled eggs.
  5. Garnish with scrambled eggs and spring onion greens. Serve hot.
